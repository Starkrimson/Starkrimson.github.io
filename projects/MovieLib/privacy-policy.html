Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025/03/20

MovieLib respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment Information:
    Not applicable as this app is free with no purchases.
  • Personal Data:
    We do not collect your name, email, or identifiable information unless voluntarily provided (e.g., feedback submissions).

2. Local Data & iCloud Sync

  • Local Storage:
    Your preferences (e.g., favorite movies, watchlists) are stored only on your device.
  • iCloud Sync:
    If enabled, data is encrypted via Apple’s iCloud service, governed by
    Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Data Control:
    • Delete local data directly in the app.
    • Manage iCloud data via device settings (Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage).

3. Third-Party Data Sources

  • This app integrates The Movie Database (TMDB) for movie and TV show information.
  • TMDB's privacy policy: https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy.
  • We do not control TMDB's data collection practices.

4. Data Security

  • Local data is encrypted by your device’s operating system (e.g., iOS Data Protection) and accessible only through the app.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data is stored until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • iCloud data follows Apple's storage policies.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: View or edit local data directly in the app.
  • Deletion: Remove local data in-app or iCloud data via device settings.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, email movielibapp@outlook.com.
